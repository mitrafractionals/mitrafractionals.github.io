Entry Services

Start with clarity. Continue with confidence.

Three audit-grade entry packages — each designed to surface the exact issues holding your business back.

01

Financial Health Audit

A comprehensive review of your financial statements over 1–3 years. We identify the top 3–5 issues silently affecting your profitability, cash flow, and financial health.

  • Branded PDF report (15–25 pages)
  • Financial scorecard with RAG indicators
  • 12-ratio analysis with benchmarks
  • 90-day prioritised action plan
  • 60-minute debrief call
  • 30-day follow-up email
02

Internal Controls Review

We assess the systems, policies, and processes your business uses to safeguard assets, ensure accuracy, and prevent fraud. Covers 8 control areas.

  • Controls assessment across 8 areas
  • RAG-rated gap analysis
  • 10-point remediation plan
  • Risk exposure summary (board-ready)
  • 60-minute debrief call
03

Audit Readiness Package

Preparing for your first statutory audit or investor due diligence? We review your files through an auditor's eye, identify what will be flagged, and coach your team to fix it first.

  • Audit readiness report (6 areas)
  • Issues log with fix guidance
  • Auditor Q&A preparation guide
  • 60-point readiness checklist
  • 60-minute debrief & coaching call
